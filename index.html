Welcome to SS Enterprises

With the specialization of manufacturing Complimentary gifts, corporate gifts, Shopping Bags, Jewellery boxes etc.


SS Enterprises is focused towards providing exclusive products with innovative designs and best quality. To name some of the products from the wide range of gift items, we manufacture Gift Pens, T-Shirts, Bags, Mugs, Key chains, Pen Drives, Calendars, Diaries, Conference Notepads, Wall & Table Clock, Desk Calendars, and Utility Products etc. We develop products as per the preference & need of the respective clients & by keeping in view the brand appeal it needs to generate with its target audience.


We specialize in catering to Jewellery Retail Segments, Gold Showrooms, and Diamond Boutiques etc. We manufacture various products such as a variety of Jewellery Boxes, Plastic Boxes, Jewellery Bags, Jute Bags, Purses, Handbags, and Gold Coin Packing Cards (specifically catering to Gold & Diamond Jewellery segments).

Whatever requirement you have, we are here for you. To fulfill all your requirements, we are just a call away. We believe in offering our clients a package of quality services leading to 100% customer satisfaction. Give us a Call or drop an email & get the ravishing experience of “PPI” with guaranteed solutions. We are available to serve you 24 x 7.
